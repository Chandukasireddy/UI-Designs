01 · Realistic Tactility

Skeuomorphism

Mimicking physical objects and real-world materials (leather, metal, glass, stitches, wood) through lighting, textures, bevels, and shadows. Highly intuitive, rich, and nostalgic.

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Bevels: Small 1px-2px inner borders simulating light catching edge.

02 · Soft Extrusion

Neomorphism

A soft, semi-realistic UI style where buttons and panels appear to be extruded from the background. Relies on dual-diagonal light and dark box-shadows matching the exact background tone.

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Background Match: Parent and element colors must be identical.
  • Shadow Angle: Usually 120 degrees (top-left light, bottom-right dark).
  • Blur & Spread: Blur should be roughly double the shadow offset distance.
  • Pressed State: Toggle to `inset` shadows to create indented presses.

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Backdrop Blur: Uses `backdrop-filter: blur(15px-30px)` to diffuse background colors.
  • Translucent Fills: Card fill is extremely light white e.g., `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.07)`.

  • Vibrancy: Deep colorful shapes behind the glass give contrast.

04 · Fluffy 3D Clay

Claymorphism

Creates a soft, inflated clay-like 3D feel using rounded/oval shapes, pastel colors, soft outer drop-shadows, and heavy layered internal shadows (light on top, dark on bottom).

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Outlines & Borders: Often uses thick light borders (`4px solid #fff`).
  • Inner Shadows: Double inner shadows to simulate 3D spheres (white top-left, dark bottom-right).
  • Outer Shadows: Long, soft, low-opacity drop shadows that float elements.
  • Colors: Saturated pastels work best to maintain the playful, toy-like tone.

05 · Pure Layout

Minimalism

Focuses on content, grid consistency, high letter-spacing contrasts, and absolute lack of decorative shadow structures or gradient noise. Uses typography and borders to separate elements.

Live Interactive Render

Functionalism dictates form. By discarding skeuomorphic simulations, glass overlays, and rounded buttons, we prioritize high-contrast typographic layouts and structural grid alignment.

Clean, sharp transitions. No round borders or shadow depth.

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Typography: Relies on clean geometric sans-serifs or elegant classic serifs.
  • Colors: Monochromatic base (black, white, grey) with at most one accent hue.
  • Borders: Crisp, solid 1px borders instead of drop-shadow offsets.
  • Grid: Perfect alignment, wide gutters, and generous negative space.

07 · Raw Neo-Brutalist Outlines

Brutalism

Neo-brutalism features raw, unpolished HTML shapes, thick black solid borders, bright high-saturation background fills, monospaced typography, and hard offset drop shadows that do not blur.

Live Interactive Render
HTML5 SNIPPET HOT 🔥

RAW COMPONENT CODE

We reject gradient blurs and glass effects. Here, shadows are solid black rectangles offset at precise angles. Fonts are raw mono.

#webdev #brutalism #css
Interactive input and click active solid state

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Outlines: Symmetrical thick black borders (usually `3px` or `4px` solid `#000000`).
  • Shadows: Hard offset shadows without blur: `box-shadow: 8px 8px 0px #000`.
  • Saturated Fills: Pure primaries (yellow, blue, orange, mint green).
  • Hover State: Cards slide onto their shadow: `transform: translate(4px, 4px); box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000;`.

08 · Fluid Organic Refractions

Liquid Glass

Combining glassmorphic frosting with organic shifting blob dimensions. Animates element borders and gradients using fluid-like keyframe distortions, creating an organic refractive appearance.

Live Interactive Render
LIQUID
89.4%

Design Parameters & Tokens

  • Border Radius Morphing: Uses complex border-radius mappings (e.g. `60% 40% 30% 70% / 60% 30% 70% 40%`).
  • Keyframe Animations: Linearly morphs between 3-4 border-radius states over 8-15 seconds.
  • Refractive Borders: Multi-layered soft white shadows paired with backdrop blurs.
  • Dynamic Lighting: Color fills change gradient orientations as the blob shifts.

Design Parameters & Tokens

Design Parameters & Tokens
